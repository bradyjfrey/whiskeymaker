Bently Heritage

Quick Run Down:
Oversight of facility design, interface with engineers, architects, construction
Project was a $150M build including 2 distilleries, a malt house, tasting room, and environmentally controlled rickhouses
Annual budget as General Manager was $12M with a total staff of 60 people
Day to day as GM: management of Sales, Marketing, Production staff, budgeting and forecasting, product launches, market management for 5 states, and state and federal compliance
As consultant I delivered the built-from-scratch LEED gold certified distillery capable of producing 200k cases/year, filling 3,000 whiskey barrels/year, trained staff, created all products, and oversaw construction and install before transitioning to a full-time position

In 2014 I was engaged as a consultant to help in the design and build of a whiskey focused distillery. The facility consisted of 2 separate distilleries, one for single malt whiskey, one for bourbon, rye, gin, and vodka. It was classified under Nevada’s estate laws so included a malting facility and 2,000 acres of grain cultivation for distillery use. It is one of the country’s only LEED gold certified distilleries for sustainable practices and reuse of historic structures.

In 2016 I accepted the role of Master Distiller and designed all products including 4 whiskies, 2 gins, a variety of liqueurs, and a vodka, all estate products. Construction began in 2015, completed in 2017, equipment install completed in 2018, and start up commenced Aug 2018.

In Jan 2021 I accepted the role of General Manager as we successfully ran a regional tasting room, expanded to 5 states and in 2022 began the process seeking a buyer for the operation. In 2023 we facilitated sale and transferred ownership at which time I made the decision to exit, taking a transition contract with the buyer, Foley Family Wines and Spirits. Having supported the transfer to management by a newly hired Master Distiller and fully trained staff, I completed my work at the site in Nov 2023.
