Death’s Door Spirits

Quick Run Down:
Standardized production for third party producers of products and trained them to the standards
Project included a $5M design-build of distillery scaled for 60k cases/year, scaled from re-launch at 12k fo DDS products to 24k cases with 25k cases of contract manufactured product
Day to day as Head Distiller included all aspects of production, physically running equipment, forecasting future needs, managed a staff of 24 distillers, QA techs, bottling staff, and production assistants
I was able to deliver the fully equipped and operating distillery, staffed and trained, forecasts and management tools

In 2011 I was asked while at Michigan State University to assist a small existing brand with product consistency and production methodology. I created standard operating procedures for their contract manufactured gin and whiskey, and in 2012 took a position as Head Distiller. We built a $5M facility, re-launched 3 products, trained a full production staff, built and managed a portfolio of contract production clients. I departed the company in 2014 leaving it with a full staff of distillers and QA techs producing at approximately 50k cases/year. The brands are still produced and sold under Dancing Goat Spirits.
